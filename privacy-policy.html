Echo privacy policy


By using Echo, Echoes website, and Echoes support server you agree to this privacy policy


What we use

We currently store information regarding to server information, user information, and music information in order to provide the best experience.

Server information

Echo currently uses and stores information for the server regarding custom prefixes and server IDs for our custom prefix panel.

User information

We store very limited amounts of user information in order to keep privacy, We currently store user IDs and usernames.View music information for more information.

Music information

We store the URL/Name of the song and the user ID/Name  of the user that request that song.


Our use of information

Our use of the information is to provide you with the best experience possible. With this being said the storing of server information is to make sure all information is correct such as correct prefixes for the server. Other information that is stored is simply there to provide you with the best experience. No information is sold.


Disclosement of information

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in the belief that such action is necessary to comply with a legal obligation, protect and defend the rights or property of the Bot or the Echo team, or protect against legal liability.


Deletion of information

Not all information may be deleted from our system.

Music information

Anything within side of the music system is automatically logged with inside of a different program We do not have the ability to delete the information with inside of these logs.

Server information

All server information will automatically be deleted upon the bot leaving the server .


Changes to policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time and from time to time without prior notice.


Contact

For any contact reasons please contact us at support@echobot.xyz and we will get back to you as soon as we can.
