Music Commands


!connect

Makes bot join vc


!play

Plays music


!resume

To resume music


!pause

To pause music


!swapdj

Swaps dj


!nowplaying

Shows current song that is playing


!queue

Shows current queue for the playlist


!equalizer

Equalizes the music


!volup

Turns volume up


!volume

Allows you to change volume with numbers


!skip

Skip's current song


!voldown

Turns volume down


!shuffle

Will shuffle playlist


Subcommands

!play_bandcamp

Will search for music on BandCamp

 

!play_twitch

Will search for music on Twitch


!play_vimeo

Will search for music on Vimeo


!play_mixer

Will search for music on Mixer


!play_http

Will search for music on Http
